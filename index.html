Project Summary

AI Study Assistant is a mobile-based learning companion designed for high school students who struggle with understanding math and science questions. Students take a photo of a question using the mobile app, the system recognizes it with OCR, and an AI engine generates guided, step-by-step explanations. Instead of simply returning the final answer, the assistant focuses on intermediate steps, hints, and conceptual reasoning tailored to the student’s level.

This project is meaningful because many existing tools encourage answer-copying rather than real learning. Our application aims to support deep understanding by offering structured, curriculum-relevant guidance on a device students already use every day: their smartphone. By combining explainable AI, question history, and simple progress summaries, AI Study Assistant helps learners build confidence in problem-solving and gives parents/teachers a safer, education-focused alternative to generic AI apps.

Key Features

  • Smart Question Capture: Students snap or upload a photo of their math or science problem; OCR extracts the content for AI processing.
  • Step-by-Step Explanations: The AI walks through each step of the solution, highlighting formulas, reasoning, and common mistakes.
  • High School Focus: Covers typical high school topics such as algebra, functions, geometry, basic calculus concepts, physics (mechanics, electricity), and introductory chemistry.
  • Adaptive Practice: Generates follow-up questions with similar difficulty to reinforce learning based on the student’s performance.
  • Progress & Topic Overview: A simple mobile dashboard shows solved questions, frequently asked topics, and suggested areas to review.

Target Audience

The primary target audience is high school students (grades 9–12) who need additional support in math and science while studying outside the classroom. The application is explicitly designed as a mobile-based solution, aiming at students who prefer to use their smartphones for homework and exam preparation.
